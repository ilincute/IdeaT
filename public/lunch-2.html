Tomato Soup

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

Calories

312.5

Calories 312.5 | Total Fat 17.1g | Saturated Fat 3.4g | Carbohydrates 35g | Fibre 9.1g | Protein 6.2g

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1-1.25kg ripe tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 large carrot
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • Fresh basil to garnish

Method

Step 1: Firstly, wash the tomatoes and cut each one into quarters slicing off any hard cores.

Step 2: Peel the onion, garlic and carrot and chop them into small pieces.

Step 3: Chop the celery into roughly the same size pieces.

Step 4: Serve with a sprinkling of torn basil leaves.
