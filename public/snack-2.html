Eggplant Chips

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

Calories 66 | Total Fat 1g | Saturated Fat 0.1g | Carbohydrates 16g | Fibre 9g | Protein 3g

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • Olive oil spray - just enough to mist the surface
  • 2 teaspoon sea salt

Method

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350° F / 180 °C.

Step 2: Slice eggplant very thin, in uniform, consistent slices.

Step 3: Bake for 20 minutes, then flip slices and bake an additional 10-20 minutes or until they begin to brown and curl up.
